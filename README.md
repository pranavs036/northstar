# NorthStar

AI-powered ecommerce optimization platform that helps brands get their products found in AI search engines — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and CoPilot/Bing AI.

## What it does

NorthStar scans your product catalog against AI search engines, identifies where competitors outrank you, and tells you exactly why — with specific, actionable fixes.

- **AI Engine Scanning** — checks product visibility across ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Bing AI
- **Competitor Analysis** — scrapes competitor PDPs and compares schema markup, content depth, and structured data
- **SKU-Level Diagnosis** — pinpoints exactly why a competitor outranks you for each query, with severity scoring
- **Audit Reports** — PDF and web dashboard with Agent-Readiness Score (0–100)
- **Maintenance Monitoring** — weekly re-scans with alerts when visibility drops
